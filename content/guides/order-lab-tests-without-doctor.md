# How to Order Lab Tests Without a Doctor

You don’t always need a doctor’s visit to access important lab tests. Here’s how to do it safely.

## Options

- **Direct-to-Consumer Lab Services**: Companies like Everlywell or LetsGetChecked allow you to order tests online.
- **Retail Labs**: Some lab chains let you schedule tests without a physician.
- **At-Home Test Kits**: Simple kits for hormones, vitamins, cholesterol, and gut health.

## Tips for Ordering Safely

1. Research reputable companies with clear privacy policies.
2. Choose tests that are clinically validated.
3. Understand what the results mean — some tests come with expert guidance.
4. Keep a copy of all results for your health tracking.

## Common Tests People Order

- Blood glucose and cholesterol
- Thyroid hormone panel
- Vitamin D and B12
- Food sensitivity and gut microbiome tests
